# downtown shared distributed services

Everbody running retail in a 'downtown' area needs the same basic services:

- store infrastructure
  - POS system
  - payment portal
  - Wireless Services (private)
  - Wireless Services (public)
  - Account management
  - Vendor management
  - Inventory management
  - Employee management
